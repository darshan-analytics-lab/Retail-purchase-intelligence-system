Retail Purchase Intelligence System

A modern intelligent shopping comparison platform that helps users compare products across multiple online marketplaces including electronics and grocery platforms.

About the Project

The Retail Purchase Intelligence System is built to simplify product research by bringing comparison data from multiple online platforms into one focused shopping experience. Users can compare electronics, groceries, and related products without manually opening several marketplaces.

The project solves the everyday problem of fragmented pricing by combining smart product categorization, price comparison, product images, platform branding, and intelligent ranking. It helps users quickly identify relevant products, compare prices, and make better purchase decisions through a modern, responsive interface.
